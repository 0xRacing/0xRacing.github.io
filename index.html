About 0xRacing

Driven by Tech

First open governance eRacing team

Using a DAO on Ethereum, 0xRacing is the first open governance racing team. Tokenized voting to add new drivers, release new merchandise and collectibles, and enable sponsorship opportunities. Blockchain based means anyone can join, without anyones permission.

Ethereum has been touted as the money of the internet (most recently with the #DeFi movement). Melding eRacing team operations with the new money of the internet creates new natural opportunities not available before.

At launch, 0xRacing is starting with multiple drivers competing in the top levels of eRacing / eSports. Most major eSport platforms do not enable in-game branding opportunities. eRacing enables each driver to use custom liveries in every single race they run, which under the 0xRacing DAO are tokenized. Meaning anyone can buy into direct in-game / in-race sponsorships. Opening up never before seen brand awareness.
